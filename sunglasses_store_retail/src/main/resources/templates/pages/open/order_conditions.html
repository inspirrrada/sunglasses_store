Warranty

The warranty for frames and sunglasses is provided for a period of 6 months, provided that the glasses are used correctly. The warranty for glasses is not provided in case of damage to the glasses that occurred as a result of:
- Careless use;
- Non-compliance with the rules of use;
- Independent replacement of part of the frame, lenses or repair;
- Physical wear and tear after the end of the warranty period.

Payment

To the item's price you have to add the shipping cost to the destination.
Available payment methods
- Advance payment to a bank account
- Cash on delivery

Return policy

You can return the glasses within 14 days from the delivery in accordance with current legislation. First of all, you have to contact with our manager, then fill out our return form and wait for our e-mail with next instructions. The product must be unused, clean and not damaged. Moreover, glasses must be returned in their original packaging with the case and all the accessories you received.
